About Us

Our goal for this project was to create a tool that allows users from anywhere in the United States to be able to find out more about their risks to COVID-19.  Using our tool will help you find out more about your own personal risk to COVID-19, while the rest of our site can help you stay up to date with current COVID-19 trends, find out more about what COVID-19 is and how you can keep yourself protected.  We strive for accuracy and clarity when delivering this information, and we hope that you found our tool useful!

This project proved to be very challenging, but it gave the team the opportunity to learn new skills and become proficient in ones they might have already had.  From the research stage all the way to the construction of this site, this project was a great learning experience.  Thank you for visiting our site!
